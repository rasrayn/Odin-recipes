Farfalle to Pesto

Ingredients:

  • Cherry Tomatoes: 134g
  • Arugula: 17g
  • Shicken breast: 151g
  • Fresh basil: 3g
  • Pine nuts: 3g
  • Parmesan cheese: 16g
  • Olive oil: 16g
  • dry pasta: 134g

Elaboration:

  1. Boil the pasta in a saucepan with water.
  2. Make the pesto
    • Put a tablespoon of oil in a bowl.
    • Chop a few basil leaves, half the garlic and the pine nuts and add them to the oil.
    • Add a teaspoon of Parmesan cheese and stir well.
  3. Cut 4 or 5 cherry tomatoes into halves or quarters.
  4. On a plate, put a layer of arugula.
  5. When the pasta is done, put it on the plate and add:
    • The tomatoes.
    • The prepared pesto sauce.
  6. cut the breast into pieces, season with salt and pepper and grill. Youcan use watever spices you want. add the cooked breast to de plate.
  7. Enjoy!
