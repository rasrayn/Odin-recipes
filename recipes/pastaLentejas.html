Dry Lentil Pasta

ingredients:

  • Red lentil helices 160g
  • Feta cheese 105g
  • Zucchini 105g
  • Yellow pepper 105g
  • Fresh basil a pinch
  • Olive oil 13g
  • Tabe salt a pinch
  • Black pepper a pinch

Elaboration:

  1. In a pot with boiling water, cook the pasta for 7-8 min.
  2. Wash and cut into small cubes:
    • Yellow pepper
    • Zucchini
  3. In a frying pan with a splash of olive oil, saut√© them.
  4. Drain the pasta and, when the vegetables are ready, add them to the pan.
  5. Serve the propellers with the vegetables on a plate.
  6. Cut the feta cheese into cubes and add it on top.
  7. Dress the propellers with:
    • A pinch of salt.
    • Black pepper.
  8. Decorate with basil and Bon appetit!
