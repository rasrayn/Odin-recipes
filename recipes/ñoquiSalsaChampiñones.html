Ñoquis con salsa de Champiñones

Ingredients:

  • Mushromms 250g
  • Onion 150g
  • Light cooking cream 200ml
  • Gnocchi 200g
  • Olive oil
  • Pepper and salt to taste

Elaboration:

  1. Brown the chopped onion with 3 tablespoons of olive oil, salt and pepper to taste, over medium heat for 5 min.
  2. Clean and slice the mushrooms, add to the pan, increase the heat to maximum power for 5 min.
  3. Add the cream, lower the heat and cook for another 5 min.
  4. Cook the gnocchi according to the manufacturer's instructions.
  5. Drain the water for gnocchi and add to the sauce.
  6. Stir, cook 2-3 minutes and that's it!
